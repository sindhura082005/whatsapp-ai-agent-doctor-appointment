# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used by the AI Agent for Doctor Appointment Booking using WhatsApp & n8n.

## Information We Collect
- Messages sent by users via WhatsApp
- Appointment details such as name, preferred date, and time

## How We Use Information
- To automate doctor appointment booking
- To send confirmations and responses via WhatsApp
- To improve user experience

## Data Protection
- User data is not shared with third parties
- Data is used only for automation purposes
- Reasonable measures are taken to protect user information

## User Consent
By using this WhatsApp service, users agree to this privacy policy.
